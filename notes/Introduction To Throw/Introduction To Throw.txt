Hello! So I will briefly explain how to get the throw stuff to work!
You will place your `o_dev_climb_throw` somewhere where the leader can
dash to / reach / bump.
To make it target a specific tile, there are two ways.

## 1.

You modify its target_x and target_y in the instance creation code.
When you trigger this code, it will find the nearest climbable block
and throw you in it. Otherwise, it will exit early!

## 2.

You set up a marker in anywhere of the room, modify its id in the
instance creation event like `m_id = 0;` and then have the thrower's
instance creation event `marker = 0;` (Marker and m_id must match!)

This will also do the same proceedure as 1.

It will look for the nearest possible climbable block.
